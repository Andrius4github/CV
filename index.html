Dr. Andrius Remeikis

is a research scientist at the Entomology Laboratory of the State Research Institute, Nature Research Centre.

Using cutting edge research facilities, including our outstanding scientific collection material, we identifying and describing numerous new taxa of Nanolepidoptera from the Neotropics for the first time revealing, analysing and incorporating data on their trophic relationships and DNA (see link: Andrius Remeikis RG profile).
